FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for a consultation?

Our initial consultation fee is BDT 5,000 for corporate matters and BDT 2,000 for personal matters. This fee is adjusted against our total professional charges if you choose to retain our services.

Do you handle cases outside Dhaka?

Yes, while our head office is in Dhaka, we represent clients across Bangladesh. We frequently travel to District Courts and High Court benches in Chittagong and Sylhet for specific cases.

What documents are required for property verification?

Typically, we require the original title deed, certified copies of previous mutation records, latest tax clearance certificate, and a recent survey map (CS/SA/RS). We can assist you in procuring these if missing.

How long does a civil lawsuit take?

The timeline varies significantly depending on the court's workload and the complexity of the case. Generally, civil suits in District Courts may take 3 to 7 years, while arbitration can be resolved within 1 to 2 years.

Can you help with company incorporation?

Absolutely. We provide end-to-end services for company incorporation, including name clearance from RJSC, drafting MoA and AoA, and obtaining the TIN and BIN certificates.
